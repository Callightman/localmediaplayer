Help for Local Media Player on Apple TV.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and choose Add Source.
  2. Jellyfin: enter your server URL, username and password.
  3. SMB / NAS: enter the host, share, username and password of your network share.
  4. Browse your library, open a title and press Play.

FAQ

Do I need a subscription? No. You can own the app forever with a one-time purchase, or choose a low monthly option. A 3-day free trial is included.

What sources are supported? Jellyfin servers and SMB/NAS network shares.

Dolby Vision / Atmos? Supported where your file and Apple TV allow; otherwise high-quality multichannel audio is used.

Subtitles? Link a free OpenSubtitles account in Settings to download subtitles; each account has its own daily quota.

Does it collect my data? No. The app collects no data and contains no ads or tracking. See our Privacy Policy.
